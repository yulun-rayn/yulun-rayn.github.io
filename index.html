I'm an applied researcher with the AI Foundations team at Capital One in the San Francisco Bay Area. My research interests include deep probabilistic modeling, causal inference, meta learning, and reinforcement learning. I earned my Ph.D. in biostatistics with D.E. in computational science and engineering at University of California, Berkeley.
